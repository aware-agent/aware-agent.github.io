What We Do

Aware Agent specializes in automating customer processes for business-to-business (B2B) engagements through a multi-agent platform. Our technology seamlessly integrates into existing business tools, enhancing operational efficiency and productivity. This solution is particularly tailored for mid to large-sized enterprises looking to optimize their internal workflows with autonomous decision-making.

Our Technology

Autonomous Decision Making

At Aware, we harness the power of state-of-the-art AI to deliver a cognitive framework where agents are equipped with advanced capabilities in planning, learning, and communication. These agents work collaboratively to handle complex tasks across various domains.

Highly Scalable

Designed for seamless scalability, Aware's solutions are ready to be deployed across enterprises of any size. Our platform not only integrates smoothly into existing infrastructures but also scales efficiently to meet growing business demands.

Robustness Through Smart Recovery

Decision-making and problem-solving capabilities drive businesses forward, and the robustness of our system is derived from its ability to autonomously identify and recover from failures. It continuously finds solutions and alternative approaches, and it proactively informs stakeholders about issues when necessary.

Collective Experiential Learning

As users engage with the system, it continuously fine-tunes and optimizes its internal processes and tool executions through a collective experiential learning process. This allows the system to adjust to company workflows without the need for human supervision.
